# Privacy Policy for Kruto Tastes

**Effective Date:** May 3, 2026

Kruto Tastes ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application (the "App") and related services.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information
When you create an account, we may collect:
*   **Identity Data**: Name, email address, and profile picture.
*   **Employment Data**: Job role, employer information, and work schedules.

### 1.2 Usage and Technical Data
*   **Device Information**: Hardware model, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
*   **Location Data**: With your permission, we collect location data specifically for clock-in and clock-out verification to ensure workplace compliance.
*   **Log Data**: Details of how you used our service, such as time-stamps of actions.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected data to:
*   Facilitate clock-in/out functionality.
*   Provide administrators with accurate reporting and payroll data.
*   Improve the App's performance and user experience.
*   Send relevant notifications regarding shifts and updates.

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure
We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:
*   **Your Employer**: To manage work schedules and payroll.
*   **Service Providers**: Third-party companies that help us provide the service (e.g., hosting, analytics).
*   **Legal Requirements**: If required by law or to protect our rights.

## 4. Security
We use industry-standard security measures (including encryption and secure storage) to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

## 5. Your Choices
*   **Location Access**: You can enable or disable location services in your device settings at any time, though this may limit your ability to clock in/out.
*   **Account Deletion**: You may request account deletion by contacting us at support@krutovodka.com.

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
